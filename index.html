Auto-update order information

If there is any change of billing address, the certain section as Shipping Method, Payment Method, Order Review or Coupon will update instantly without reloading the entire page, that depends on Trigger Configuration in the backend. Moreover, customers can change the items if they need such as add or subtract their quantity, add new products even remove the current one. All of them are carried out instantly on the order review section.
